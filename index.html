About IEEE

Vision: IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

Mission: IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

IEEE is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

About IEEE SB STM

The IEEE Student Branch at St. Thomas College of Engineering and Technology, Kannur (IEEE SB STM), established in December 2023, is a vibrant student-led community driven by innovation, learning, and impact.

Guided by our Branch Counsellor Dr. Sreekesh Namboodiri T, the branch provides opportunities for students to explore technology beyond classrooms through workshops, tech talks, hackathons, competitions, and collaborative projects.
